Free Finance Calculators

This website provides free and easy-to-use finance calculators for students and beginners. All calculators are designed to help you perform basic financial calculations quickly and accurately.

These tools are useful for loan planning, savings estimation, investment understanding, and basic financial education.

Available Calculators

  • Simple Interest Calculator – Calculate interest using the simple interest formula.
  • Compound Interest Calculator – Calculate compound interest and investment growth over time.
  • EMI Calculator – Calculate monthly loan EMI for home, car, and personal loans.
  • SIP Calculator – Calculate future value of monthly SIP investments.
  • FD Calculator – Calculate fixed deposit maturity amount.
  • RD Calculator – Calculate recurring deposit maturity value.
  • Loan Eligibility Calculator – Estimate loan affordability based on income.
  • Savings Growth Calculator – Calculate future value of savings.
  • Inflation Calculator – Understand how inflation affects money value.
  • Percentage Calculator – Calculate percentage values easily.

Who Should Use These Calculators

  • Students learning finance basics
  • Beginners planning savings and investments
  • People calculating loans and EMIs
  • Anyone needing quick financial calculations

More calculators will be added in the future to cover additional financial needs.
